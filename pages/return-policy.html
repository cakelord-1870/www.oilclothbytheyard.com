DISCLAIMER / RETURN POLICY:

Please note as of January 1, 2020 all refunds will be given less a additional 5% fee - we apologize in advance, all credit card processors and Paypal do not refund the fees they charge us if a refund is issued. We have to pass this expense on to the customer. We highly suggest if you have changed your mind about a print and want something else (prior to order being processed), that you call us. So we can adjust your original order. If you go ahead and place a second order, rather than allowing us to adjust the original order, we will charge a 5% fee. This is to cover the cost that Paypal and all credit card processors are charging.

 

 

Because of the methods used in manufacturing oilcloth, you will find variations such as squiggles, speckles, wrinkles & off register printing which is normal, inherent and may occur. This will not affect the quality & durability of the oilcloth and is not considered cause for return. Color lots may vary. Product is sold as is. All products are wipe clean with soapy sponge, not machine washable. No dryer, no iron. The color of fabric in the printed and web-based swatch catalog may vary from the actual fabric color depending on your printer and computer monitor. No returns on cut fabric. Our fabric is not a waxed fabric. Our fabric does not contain linseed oil. We accept returns and exchanges on unopened unused rolls of fabric. For rolls  Please call us within 24 hours of receipt for RA/EX number. All returns must be made within 10 days of receipt of merchandise. No returns accepted without this number. Any claim made for damaged product must include customer sending images of damage as well as original packaging, and no exceptions. Returns accepted on unopened, uncut rolls of fabric are subject to the following: 30% restocking fee, no refund on shipping, you pay return freight. On rolls that are shipped folded in a box, the restocking fee is 50%. We reserve the right to refuse any returns at any time.

 

All Christmas prints are final sale! No returns, refunds or exchanges.

All sale items are final sale!
